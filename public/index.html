M-Core Rootstocks

Composition templates exposed to the M-Core method
Rootstocks are reusable behavior-tree composition templates exposed to the M-Core method during synthesis. They are not executable skills themselves; instead, they provide canonical structural patterns that the agent can instantiate with the correct objects, locations, and control-flow slots for a task.
